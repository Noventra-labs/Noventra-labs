Operating system

Four protocols from observed signal to validated release.

01 / SOURCE

Collect recurring pain

We gather complaints across communities and normalize them into comparable problem statements.

02 / VERIFY

Measure demand strength

Each gap needs confidence thresholds before build approval: frequency, urgency, and solution dissatisfaction.

03 / SHIP

Build openly from day one

Public repos, transparent milestones, and community check-ins keep progress honest and useful.

04 / EVOLVE

Iterate with live usage

Roadmaps change based on behavior data and direct user contribution, not internal narratives.
